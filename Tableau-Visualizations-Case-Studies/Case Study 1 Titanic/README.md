#  Case Study 1 – Titanic Dataset

###  Objective
To analyze Titanic passenger data and understand how factors like gender, age, and passenger class affected survival chances.

---

###  Dataset
`titanic.csv`

---

###  Visualizations Performed
1. **Bar Chart:** Passengers vs. Gender  
2. **Stacked Bar Chart:** Survival Count by Gender  
3. **Bar Chart:** Survival Rate by Passenger Class  
4. **Histogram:** Age Distribution of Passengers  
5. **Bar Chart:** Average Fare by Class  
6. **Scatter Plot:** Fare vs. Age  
7. **Bar Chart:** Passenger Count by Embarkation Port  
8. **Heatmap:** Survival by Gender and Class  
9. **Box Plot:** Age vs. Survival  
10. **Pie Chart:** Gender Distribution  

---

###  Key Insights
- **Female passengers in 1st Class** had the highest survival rate (~97%).  
- **3rd Class males** had the lowest survival chance.  
- **Cherbourg port** passengers showed higher survival rates.  
- **Fare and class** showed a strong positive correlation with survival.  
- **Most passengers** were between 20–40 years old.
